Bugatti Bolide

Track-focused hypercar with extreme performance

About the Bolide

The Bugatti Bolide is a high-performance concept car with a quad-turbo W16 engine. Built purely for the track, it offers an unmatched power-to-weight ratio and radical aerodynamic design.

  • Engine: 8.0L W16 quad-turbo
  • Power: 1,850 HP
  • Top Speed: 310+ mph (claimed)
  • Weight: 1,240 kg
  • 0-100 km/h: ~2.17 seconds
